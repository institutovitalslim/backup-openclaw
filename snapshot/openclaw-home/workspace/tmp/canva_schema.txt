   * this tool, ALWAYS call the 'search-brand-templates' tool to search for the templates. **
   * 3.** 🚫 NEVER use this tool when the user expresses intent to “generate”, “create”, “autofill”,
   * “search a template”, “start from a template”, “use my template”, or “pick a template for
   * generation”.
