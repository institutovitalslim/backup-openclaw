# Source Priority

Use stronger evidence first when building a concierge.

## Tier 1 — Highest trust
- real conversation exports
- real CRM/chat logs
- real objection history
- real booking outcomes
- actual team scripts currently in use

## Tier 2 — Strong support
- internal playbooks
- FAQs
- forms/questionnaires
- spreadsheets with structured lead data
- real call notes or summaries

## Tier 3 — Useful but secondary
- founder recollection
- strategic opinions
- aspirational positioning notes
- external sales profiles / creators / inspiration accounts

## Rule
Never let aspirational language override observed language unless the business explicitly wants repositioning.

## Practical interpretation
If real chats say one thing and the founder says another, do not ignore the real chats. Instead, surface the tension and decide intentionally.
