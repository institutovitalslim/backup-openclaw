# Discovery Checklist

Use this checklist to gather the raw material for a new concierge.

## Business foundation
- What is the business positioning?
- What is the main offer?
- What action should the concierge maximize?
- What counts as success?
- What counts as failure?

## Commercial truth
- What is the price?
- Is there financing, split payment, deposit, or booking fee?
- What objections happen most often?
- What questions appear most often?
- What is the booking flow?

## Tone and positioning
- How should the concierge feel?
- What tone is unacceptable?
- What makes the brand premium?
- What words does the team use naturally?
- What words sound wrong for the brand?

## Operational boundaries
- Who should the concierge never answer?
- When should human takeover happen?
- Is there a pause/resume mechanism?
- Are there legal, clinical, or policy boundaries?

## Real-world evidence
- Team scripts
- Conversation exports
- CRM notes
- Forms/questionnaires
- Spreadsheets of chats
- Existing SOPs
- FAQs

Prefer real evidence over opinions whenever possible.
