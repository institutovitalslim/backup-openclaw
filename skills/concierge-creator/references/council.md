# Council Guide

Use a council review before calling a concierge ready.

## Purpose
Force multi-angle review so the concierge is not approved only by the person who built it.

## Review lenses
Run at least these lenses:
- commercial / conversion
- copy / WhatsApp naturalness
- safety / clinical or policy boundaries when relevant
- premium positioning / brand fit
- operator / production-readiness

## What each lens should look for

### Commercial / conversion
- Is the concierge actually moving the lead toward the target action?
- Is it missing chances to progress?
- Is it too passive?
- Is it too pushy?
- Does it build value before asking for commitment?

### Copy / WhatsApp naturalness
- Does it sound like a real person from the business?
- Is the cadence repetitive?
- Are messages too long?
- Are questions too list-like?
- Does it feel scripted?

### Safety / boundaries
- Is there clinical overreach?
- Is it promising results it should not promise?
- Is it making unsupported claims?
- Is it stepping into policy-sensitive areas?

### Premium positioning / brand fit
- Does it feel elegant?
- Does it protect trust?
- Does it avoid telemarketing energy?
- Does it avoid generic AI tone?
- Does it feel aligned with a high-touch concierge brand?

### Production-readiness
- Are controls defined?
- Is there pause/resume logic?
- Is there human takeover?
- Is there a QA battery?
- Is there a learning loop?

## Typical failure signals
- friendly funnel energy
- institutional excess
- evasive pricing
- too much validation before movement
- manipulative urgency
- over-questioning the lead
- weak next-step guidance

## Output standard
The council output should end with one of these:
- approve
- approve with adjustments
- not ready

Then record:
- strongest advantages
- biggest risks
- what must change before launch
- what can wait for pre-production monitoring
